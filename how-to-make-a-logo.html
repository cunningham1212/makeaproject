What Logo Is Right For You?

The Wordmark (FedEx, Google, Coca Cola): Depending what font you use, it can help make your user feel like the brand has a history.

Pictorial (Apple, Target): Good for generic names that have images directly or indirectly associated with them.

Abstract Iconography (Pepsi, Nike): All else, go here.

Picking The Colors

Here is the rule, you are allowed to use as much white, black and gray as you want, but keep it to at most, two colors. Obviously pick the colors that make the most sense to you.
