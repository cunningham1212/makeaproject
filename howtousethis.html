What even is...this

It's not really just a club or a website, it is a commitment - do something you are passionate about every week. That doesn't sound radical, but how often do you consistently do that?


If you already have a thing you know how to do and want to do it, well, you're ready to start. But if you don't know where to start or even what to do, that doesn't mean abandon ship. We've got your back with a thing we call resources.
